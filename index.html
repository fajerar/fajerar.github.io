Aim & Problem Statement

Research aim Is enhancing cybersecurity awareness in Secondary school student​.

The shift to online learning in the Bahrain has left students with low cybersecurity awareness.​This increases vulnerability to phishing, identity theft, financial fraud, and other cyber threats.​Current awareness methods are traditional and ineffective for young learners.​MACS is proposed as an interactive mobile app to close this gap.​The app strengthens student awareness through engaging activities and real-time safety alerts.
