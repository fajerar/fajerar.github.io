Development of a Mobile Application for Educational Cyber Security Awareness (MACS)

Enhancing cybersecurity awareness among secondary school students through interactive learning

Explore the App

About the Project

MACS (Mobile Application for Educational Cyber Security Awareness) is a mobile-based solution designed to improve cybersecurity awareness among secondary school students.

Problem Statement

  • Many people in Bahrain have been victims of online scams.
  • Students have low cybersecurity awareness due to traditional learning methods.
  • There is a need for an interactive and engaging educational solution.

Aim & Objectives

Aim:

Enhance cybersecurity awareness among secondary school students.

Objectives:

  • Educate students about cyber threats
  • Promote safe online behavior
  • Use interactive learning methods

Application Overview

MACS simplifies cybersecurity concepts using interactive content, allowing students to learn through real-world scenarios and activities.

Key Features

  • AI Chatbot for instant cybersecurity guidance
  • Scenario-based quizzes
  • Online team multiplayer learning
  • Real-time safety awareness

Technology & Development

  • Mobile-based application
  • Agile-based development approach
  • Compatible with multiple operating systems
  • User-focused interface

Conclusion & Future Work

MACS enhances cybersecurity awareness through interactive learning. Future work includes personalized avatars and expanded features.

Project Contribution

  • Developed a mobile-based educational application
  • Employed multiple questionnaires
  • Prepared research article for academic publication
